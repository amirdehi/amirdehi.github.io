Flashlight + Screenlight

Try a different lighting experience by using the Flashlight controller! This app not only allows you to turn the Flashlight on and off, but also provides access to a variety of exciting light patterns. From light dances inspired by popular music and themes to unique light modes such as disco and zigzag light dance, this app brings you a fascinating collection of light patterns. Whether you are on the road in the middle of the night and looking for help, or you are looking for excitement in a friendly group, this app will always be needed. You can use the various effects of this program in two different modes of Flashlight and screen light. Enjoy the light dance!
