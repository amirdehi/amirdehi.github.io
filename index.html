About Me

My name is Ali Ramezanpour Amirdehi, a 25-year-old software engineer currently working at NRDC in Tehran, Iran. I am on a journey to become a full-stack developer, driven by a passion for challenges and the excitement of trying new things. My career began with a love for photography during my school years, but my focus shifted to programming when I entered university.

With over 3.5 years of professional experience in Java web programming, I've honed my skills in various areas of software development. My expertise extends beyond web development, encompassing image processing, Android application development, Unity game creation, and more. Each project I undertake is a new opportunity to innovate and push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world.
